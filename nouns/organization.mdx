# Organization

An **Organization** is a structured group of people working together toward common goals. Organizations can be companies, non-profits, government agencies, or other institutional entities.

## Properties

### Core Properties

- **name**: The official registered name
- **legalName**: Legal name if different from display name
- **url**: Official website
- **logo**: Brand logo image

### Contact Information

- **email**: Primary contact email
- **telephone**: Main phone number
- **address**: Physical location

### Classification

- **industry**: NAICS industry classification
- **organizationType**: Type (corporation, LLC, non-profit, etc.)
- **foundingDate**: When the organization was established

## Relationships

Organizations can have relationships with:

- **employees**: People who work for the organization
- **members**: Members (for associations)
- **parentOrganization**: Larger organization this is part of
- **subOrganization**: Smaller organizations within this one
- **founders**: People who founded the organization
